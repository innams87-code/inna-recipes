Parotta

Prep 20 min + rest • Cook 15 min • Serves 4 • Level: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp sugar, 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp oil + more for cooking
  • ~3/4 cup warm water (as needed)

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, salt. Add water gradually and 1 tbsp oil to form a soft dough. Knead 5–7 min.
  2. Coat with 1 tbsp oil. Rest 20–30 min.
  3. Divide into balls. Roll each very thin, brush oil, fold into long strip, then coil (like a snail).
  4. Rest coils 10 min. Flatten gently into discs.
  5. Cook on medium pan with a little oil until golden spots both sides. Fluff by clapping lightly. Serve hot.
