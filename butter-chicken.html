Butter Chicken

Prep 15 min • Cook 25 min • Serves 4 • Level: Easy

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken thighs, cubed
  • 2 tbsp butter + 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp chili powder (to taste)
  • 200g tomato puree
  • 150 ml cream (or coconut cream)
  • Salt, pepper, fresh coriander

Steps

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Sear in oil + 1 tbsp butter until lightly browned. Remove.
  2. In the same pan, add 1 tbsp butter. Sauté onion 3–4 min, then garlic + ginger 30 sec.
  3. Add garam masala and chili; stir 30 sec. Add tomato puree, simmer 5–7 min.
  4. Stir in cream. Return chicken, simmer 10–12 min until cooked and sauce thickens.
  5. Finish with a knob of butter and coriander. Serve with parotta or rice.
