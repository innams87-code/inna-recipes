Chicken Biryani

Prep 20 min • Cook 35–40 min • Serves 4 • Level: Medium

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, bone-in or boneless
  • 2 cups basmati rice (soaked 20 min)
  • 2 onions (sliced), 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala, 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chili
  • Whole spices: bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom
  • Mint + coriander, ginger-garlic paste
  • Yogurt 1/2 cup, ghee/oil, salt

Steps

  1. Parboil soaked rice with salt and whole spices until 70% done; drain.
  2. In a pot, heat ghee/oil; fry onions till golden. Add ginger-garlic; sauté.
  3. Add chicken, spices, tomatoes, yogurt; cook till chicken is 80–90% done.
  4. Layer: chicken gravy at bottom → rice → herbs. Repeat if needed.
  5. Seal and steam (dum) on low 15–20 min. Rest 5 min, fluff, serve hot.
